Requirements:

  • 5+ years of full-stack development experience
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and Python
  • Experience with React, Next.js, and modern frontend frameworks
  • Knowledge of PDF processing libraries (PyMuPDF, pdf-lib, etc.)
