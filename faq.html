Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RocketPDF

Security & Privacy

Are my files safe and secure?
Yes! Your files are processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. We never upload your files to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. All processing happens on your device.
Do you store my files?
No, we do not store any of your files. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Once you close the page, all data is automatically cleared from your browser's memory.
Is RocketPDF GDPR compliant?
Yes, RocketPDF is fully GDPR compliant. We collect minimal data (only anonymous analytics), never store your files, and respect your privacy rights. See our GDPR page for more details.

Using RocketPDF Tools

Is RocketPDF really free?
Yes! All RocketPDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations. We believe everyone should have access to quality PDF tools.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required! You can use all RocketPDF tools instantly without signing up. Simply select your tool, upload your file, and start working.
What file size limit do you have?
Since all processing happens in your browser, the file size limit depends on your device's memory. Most modern devices can handle files up to 100MB without issues. For very large files, we recommend using a desktop computer.
Can I use RocketPDF on mobile devices?
Yes! RocketPDF is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. However, for the best experience with large files, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer.

PDF Tools

How do I compress a PDF without losing quality?
Use our Compress PDF tool and select the "High Quality" option. This will reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. For maximum compression, choose "Maximum Compression" but note that some quality loss may occur.
Can I merge multiple PDFs at once?
Yes! Our Merge PDF tool supports multiple file uploads. Simply drag and drop all your PDF files, arrange them in the desired order, and click merge.
How accurate is the PDF to Word conversion?
Our PDF to Word converter maintains formatting, fonts, and layout with high accuracy. However, complex layouts or scanned PDFs may require some manual adjustments after conversion.
Can I password protect my PDFs?
Yes! Use our Lock PDF tool to add password protection to your documents. Note that true encryption requires server-side processing, so our current version provides a demo interface.
