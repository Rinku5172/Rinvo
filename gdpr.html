General Data Protection Regulation

Our Commitment to GDPR

RocketPDF is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We take data protection seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to ensure compliance.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing

How We Protect Your Data

No File Storage

Your files are processed in your browser and are never stored on our servers. Files are automatically deleted after processing.

Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL protocols.

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide our services.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data, primarily for analytics purposes to improve our service. This may include:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device information
