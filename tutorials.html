Tips & Best Practices

✅ Use Batch Processing

Save time by processing multiple files at once instead of one by one.

✅ Preview Before Download

Always preview your processed PDF to ensure it meets your expectations.

✅ Choose the Right Compression

Use Maximum for emails, Recommended for general use, and High Quality for printing.

✅ Your Privacy Matters

All processing happens locally in your browser - we never store your files.
